Our Vision

A professional and high performance football academy designed to develop, improve and market the registered football player in every facet of the sporting fraternity on a national, regional and international scale.

Our Mission

To professionalise the industry of sport turning it into an organised, high performance arena, establishing Galaxy Football Academy as a leader in producing tomorrow's football stars today.

Our Focus

The creation of strategies thst ensure efficiency, accountability, transparency and professionalism in the developing of young football talent from all walks of life through introducing them to our values of athletic participation.

Our Mainstay

Our mainstay is the deliberate move to bring young talent from different cultures and backgrounds together creating opportunities to develop their football skills in an all inclusive and tolerant environment developed and regulated by professionals in the sporting fraternity.

  • To afford registered athletes opportunities to pursue their football dreams outside the formal school arena.
  • To identify, develop and expose young talent through registering them with a specialised football talent development program.
  • To develop the talent base and create a wider pool of players for the nation and the world at large.
  • To offer young athletes a healthy lifestyle away from drugs and socially destructive behaviour.
  • To establish mutually beneficial patnerships with all stakeholders in the community and corporate world thus creating positive momentum towards the developement of the individual, the community and the nation through sport.
  • Cultivation of good morals and good habits.
  • Developing a sense of responsibility for one's future.
  • Learning the reward of determination, dependability and honesty.
  • Learning acceptable reactions to pressure, success and adversity.
  • Develop and attitude of tolerance and inclusion.
  • Develop self-confidence and improve individual skills.
  • Obtaining tangible opportunities for a scholarship or a professional football player's contract.
  • To participate in outreach programs that teach awareness of social and cultural differences and social responsibility.
  • Experience the principles of fair play both on and off the field of play.
  • Experience the joy and value of Christianity.
  • The society values the development of future citizens that are productive.
  • It is easy to teach positive values to the young through sport.
  • A number of boys and girls love football and desire to be like the current stars e.g. Messi, Ronaldo, Rooney
  • To offer young athletes a healthy lifestyle away from drugs and socially destructive behaviour.
  • Zimbabwe needs professional academies like Galaxy Football Academy to create tomorrow's stars.
